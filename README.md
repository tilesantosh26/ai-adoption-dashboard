# Global AI Adoption & Business Impact Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** analyzing global trends in **Artificial Intelligence (AI) adoption across industries and countries**.

The dashboard highlights how AI adoption is growing across industries, how AI investments impact productivity, and how adoption varies across different countries.

This project demonstrates **data visualization, business intelligence, and analytical skills using Microsoft Power BI**.

# 🔍 Key Insights

### AI Adoption is Increasing
The **AI Adoption Growth by Industry** chart shows a steady increase in adoption from **2023 to 2026** across industries.

### Technology and Finance Lead Adoption
The **Industry-wise AI Adoption Comparison** chart shows that **Technology, Finance, and Logistics sectors** have the highest AI adoption rates.

### AI Investment Improves Productivity
The **Impact of AI Budget on Productivity** scatter plot shows a positive relationship between **AI investment and productivity growth**.

### Global AI Adoption Varies by Region
The **Country-wise AI Adoption Distribution map** shows how AI adoption differs across countries.

# 📊 Dashboard Components

## 1️⃣ AI Adoption Growth by Industry
**Visualization:** Line Chart

Shows how AI adoption has increased across industries from **2023–2026**.

Purpose:
- Track AI growth trends
- Compare industries over time

---

## 2️⃣ Industry-wise AI Adoption Comparison
**Visualization:** Clustered Column Chart

Compares AI adoption rates across industries for multiple years.

Purpose:
- Identify industries leading AI adoption
- Compare yearly changes

---

## 3️⃣ Impact of AI Budget on Productivity
**Visualization:** Scatter Plot

Shows the relationship between **AI investment and productivity improvement**.

Purpose:
- Evaluate ROI of AI investments
- Identify productivity patterns

---

## 4️⃣ Country-wise AI Adoption Distribution
**Visualization:** Map

Displays geographic distribution of AI adoption by country.

Purpose:
- Identify regions with higher AI adoption
- Analyze global AI trends

# 🗂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains the following fields:

| Column | Description |
|------|------|
| Country | Country where the data was collected |
| Industry | Business sector |
| Year | Year of observation |
| AI_Adoption_Percent | Percentage of companies adopting AI |
| Avg_AI_Budget_Percent | Average AI investment percentage |
| Productivity_Change | Productivity improvement due to AI |
| Companies_Using_AI | Number of companies using AI |

# 🚀 How to Use

1. Download the `.pbix` file from the repository.
2. Open it in **Microsoft Power BI Desktop**.
3. Use slicers to filter by:
   - Country
   - Industry
   - Year
4. Explore the dashboard to analyze AI adoption patterns.
